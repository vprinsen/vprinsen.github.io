Mushroom soup

Ingredients:

  • 1.5L water
  • 1 cup vegetable or mushroom bouillon
  • 225g fresh mushrooms (white or cremini), roughly chopped or sliced
  • 1/2 cup dried mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1/3 cup sour cream or plain yogurt
  • salt to taste
Serve with: pasta or kluski

Preparation:

  1. Add water, bouillon cube, and dried mushrooms to a saucepan and bring to a boil. Simmer for 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until dried mushrooms are soft.
  2. In parallel, in a frying pan, sauté fresh mushrooms with some butter or oil for 5-10 minutes until cooked through. (Tips: https://www.inspiredtaste.net/36274/how-to-cook-mushrooms/)
  3. Turn down the heat on the broth and fish out the softened dried mushrooms. Chop them, and the fresh mushrooms, into small pieces.
  4. In a tall-sided container (a large measuring cup works well), combine flour and sour cream with 1-2 ladles of soup and half the chopped mushrooms using a hand blender. Pour resulting liquid back into the soup and stir until combined and warmed through.
  5. Add remaining chopped mushrooms directly to the soup. (Alternately, if you prefer a fully smooth soup, you can blend all the mushrooms in the step above.)
  6. Salt to taste.
