Tomato soup

Ingredients:

  • 1.5L water
  • 1 cube vegetable or chicken bouillon
  • 1 can (56mL/5.5 fl oz) tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1/3 cup sour cream or plain yogurt
  • salt to taste
Serve with: rice, kluski, or tortellini (for a more filling meal)

Preparation:

  1. Combine water, bouillon cube, and tomato paste in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Simmer for 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until ingredients are well combined.
  2. In a tall-sided container (a large measuring cup works well), combine flour and sour cream with 1-2 ladles of soup using a hand blender or mixer.
  3. Turn down the heat and pour combined liquid back into the soup and stir until combined and warmed through.
  4. Salt to taste.
