Nancy's PUMPKIN PIE Temperature: 400 F. - 15 minutes 350 F. - 25 minutes 2 eggs, slightly beaten 2/3 cup brown sugar, tightly packed 1 1/2 cups canned or cooked pumpkin 1 teaspoon cinnamon 1/4 teaspoon cloves 1/4 teaspoon ginger 1/2 teaspoon salt 1 cup milk 1/2 cup cereal cream Combine slightly beaten eggs with sugar, pumpkin, spices, salt and milk and cream. Line 9" pie pan with plain pastry; flute on edge. Bake in hot oven (450 F) for 5 minutes. Pour pumpkin mixture into partially baked shell. Bake in hot oven for 10 minutes. Reduce heat to 350 F. Continue baking until filling is set (knife inserted into centre comes out clean.
