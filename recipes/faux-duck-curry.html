"Duck" curry

Ingredients:

400mL coconut milk 1-2 cans veggie duck* 2 tbsp red curry paste 1 tbsp dark soy sauce 1 tbsp light soy sauce 1/2 tbsp maple syrup (original recipe calls for 15g palm sugar dissolved in 1 tbsp boiled water) 3 kaffir lime leaves, torn 12 lychees 1/4 fresh pineapple, chunked, or ~200mL canned pineapple chunks (drained of syrup) 8 small cherry tomatoes Thai basil leaves *Where to find veggie duck? You can find canned "mock duck" in most Asian groceries: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mock_duck In Montreal, you can also find it frozen at Paradis Vegetarien: https://www.paradisvegetarien.com/product-page/veg-o-mix-seitan-duck-flavor-v%C3%A9g%C3%A9-mix-saveur-de-canard-seitan The package comes with 2 pieces, I use one for this recipe.
